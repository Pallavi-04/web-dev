Takshshila Campus

The College offers four years Undergraduate Course in different streams which include Computer Science & Engineering; Electronics & Communication Engineering; Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The college does not have any programs for the Post Graduate students. The yearly intake of the students in the various courses is as per the limit specified by the AICTE. In computer Science the intake is 90, in Electrical & Electronics its 60, in Electronics and Communication its 120 and its 60 in Mechanical Engineering.

Facilities

The Library has large volumes of text and reference books on a variety of subjects: Computer Science, Physics, Electronics, Mathematics, Management, Commerce, Physical Education, Literature, Educational Books. Apart from books, students have access to the latest Magazines, International Journals, Encyclopedia Britannica and other, Newspapers and Research papers. One book can be borrowed for a period of twentyfive days on one library ticket. Besides, another reading material on various topics is provided. The college has sports facilities for all students. About Us

Takshshila Institute of Engineering & Technology was established in 2004 by R.N.K. Education Society. It is placed among the best in professional education in Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya. It is determined to provide world class quality education to students. It offers five undergraduate and six postgraduate courses in the field of engineering and technology. It also offers management programmes. It is located at Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Technical University, Bhopal It is accredited by NBA, New Delhi and an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Institution Department
